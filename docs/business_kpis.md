# Business KPI Layer

The mart layer is designed to answer realistic ERP executive questions.

## Finance
- Net revenue by month, currency and customer segment
- Revenue leakage from cancelled or delayed orders
- Top customers by trailing 90-day revenue

## Customer 360
- First order date, latest order date and lifetime value
- Active/inactive customer classification
- Regional visibility controlled by row access policy

## Operations
- Late shipment rate by region and product family
- Order status aging and backlog trend
- Inventory and fulfillment extensions can be added through the same raw-to-vault-to-mart pattern

## Trust Metrics
- Source freshness SLA
- Row-count reconciliation between raw, staging and marts
- Data quality pass rate and failed checks by severity
